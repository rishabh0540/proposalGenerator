The Working Model

Seamless "Bill-to-Ship-to" Execution

1

Order Alignment

Buyer confirms rate/qty with vendors; aligns with Poshn.

2

Billing Structure

Vendor bills Poshn; Poshn counter-bills the Buyer.

3

Settlement

Poshn pays 100% to vendor on 3rd day of quality report.

4

Final Closure

Buyer pays Poshn after agreed payment days from GRN.

Operational Note: 10% margin money provided by the Buyer to Poshn upon vehicle delivery.
